Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Our Promise: Matchic processes everything on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data, wardrobe photos, or outfit information. Your data belongs to you and stays on your phone.

1. Introduction

Matchic ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Matchic handles your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Matchic is an AI-powered wardrobe management app that runs entirely on your device. We have designed our app with a privacy-first architecture — all data processing, including AI inference, happens locally on your device using on-device machine learning models.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Because of our on-device architecture, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Wardrobe data (photos of clothing items, tags, categories, colors)
  • Outfit combinations or lookbook entries
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports containing personal data
  • Biometric data

3. Information We Process Locally

The following data is processed exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Photos of clothing items: Captured via camera or imported from your photo library. These are stored in the app's local database (ObjectBox) and your device's local storage.
  • Clothing metadata: Tags, categories, colors, patterns, materials, and style information generated by AI or entered manually.
  • Outfit recommendations: AI-generated suggestions based on your wardrobe — the AI model runs locally via MediaPipe.
  • App preferences: Language, theme, and display settings stored locally via SharedPreferences.

4. Permissions We Request

Matchic requires certain device permissions to function. Each permission is used only for its stated purpose:

Camera (iOS: NSCameraUsageDescription)

Used exclusively when you actively take a photo of a clothing item. We do not access the camera in the background. Photos are processed immediately and stored locally.

Photo Library (iOS: NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription)

Used only when you choose to import existing photos into the app. You control which photos are imported. We never access your entire photo library without your explicit action.

Internet Access

Matchic does not require internet access for core functionality. The app works fully offline. Internet access may be used only for optional features such as downloading AI model updates when you explicitly choose to do so.

5. AI & On-Device Processing

Matchic uses Google's MediaPipe framework with Gemma 3 models to provide AI features entirely on your device:

  • Auto-tagging: When you add a clothing item, the AI analyzes the image locally to suggest colors, categories, and styles.
  • Outfit recommendation: The AI analyzes your wardrobe locally to suggest outfit combinations.
  • No cloud AI: All machine learning inference happens on your device. No images or data are sent to external AI services.
  • Model download: The AI model (~275MB) is downloaded from Google's official repository only when you explicitly choose to enable AI features. The model itself does not transmit any data.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • ObjectBox: A high-performance local NoSQL database for structured data (items, tags, outfits).
  • Local file storage: Images are stored in the app's private directory on your device's internal storage.
  • SharedPreferences: For app settings and preferences.

Data is not encrypted at rest by the app itself; it is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, file-based encryption on modern Android/iOS devices).

7. Data Backup & Export

Matchic provides you with the ability to export your entire wardrobe (photos + data) as a ZIP file through the Backup & Restore feature. This file is saved to your device and is under your control. We do not have access to backup files.

You can delete all app data at any time through the Settings screen ("Clear All Data") or by uninstalling the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Matchic is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is retained locally on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You can:

  • Delete individual items: Remove specific clothing items, tags, or outfits directly in the app at any time.
  • Clear all data: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings to erase everything at once. This action is irreversible.
  • Uninstall the app: All locally stored data is automatically removed when you uninstall Matchic. We recommend exporting a backup first if you wish to retain your data.

Since we do not collect or store your data on any servers, there is no data held by us to delete. Your data remains entirely on your device and under your control.

10. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA & International)

To the extent applicable, you have the following rights regarding your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most rights directly within the app:

Right to Access

All your data is accessible directly within the app — browse your closet, view items, tags, and lookbook. There is no server-side data to request from us because nothing is collected.

Right to Rectification

You can edit item details (name, category, color, tags, notes) directly within the app at any time.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

You can delete individual items, clear all data via Settings, or uninstall the app. No further action is needed from us as we hold none of your data.

Right to Data Portability

Use the Backup & Restore feature to export your entire wardrobe (photos + data) as a ZIP file, fully under your control and usable across devices.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold. Matchic does not collect, sell, or share any personal information as defined under the CCPA. There is no personal information to disclose because no data leaves your device.

International Users & Cross-Border Data Transfers

Matchic does not transfer any personal data across national borders. All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. There is no data to transfer internationally, and no data leaves your phone under any circumstances.

11. Third-Party Services & Licenses

Matchic uses the following open-source libraries and frameworks, all of which operate locally on your device:

  • Flutter & Dart: Cross-platform app framework (BSD 3-Clause License)
  • ObjectBox: Local NoSQL database (ObjectBox License)
  • MediaPipe GenAI: On-device AI inference (Apache License 2.0)
  • Gemma 3 Model: On-device AI model (Gemma Terms of Use)
  • AutoRoute: In-app navigation (MIT License)
  • Google Fonts: Local font rendering (SIL Open Font License)
  • flutter_liquid_glass: UI effects (MIT License)
  • image_picker: Photo selection (BSD 3-Clause License)

None of these libraries transmit your data externally. Matchic does not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or third-party tracking services of any kind.

When you choose to download the AI model (~275MB), the download occurs directly from Google's official repository via standard HTTPS. This is a straightforward file download — no data about you or your wardrobe is included or transmitted during this process. The model file itself contains no data collection mechanisms.

12. Data Security

Your data is protected at multiple levels:

  • Device-level encryption: Modern iOS and Android devices use file-based encryption (FBE), which encrypts app data at rest automatically.
  • App sandboxing: Matchic operates within the standard iOS/Android app sandbox, preventing other apps from accessing your wardrobe data.
  • No network transmission: Since no data is transmitted over the network, there is zero risk of interception during transit.
  • Local-only storage: Photos, metadata, and preferences are stored exclusively in the app's private directory, inaccessible to other applications.

For additional protection, we recommend securing your device with a strong passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, or biometric lock.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through one or more of the following:

  • In-app notification or prompt
  • Notice on our website (matchic.app)
  • Email notification (if you have chosen to provide your email)

Your continued use of Matchic after the effective date of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Contact & Data Protection Inquiries

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, your data, or applicable privacy rights (including GDPR, CCPA, or other jurisdiction-specific inquiries), please contact us:

  • Email: support@matchic.app
  • GitHub: github.com/matchic
  • Response time: We aim to respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days as required by applicable regulations.

For urgent data protection matters, please use "Data Privacy Request" as your email subject line for priority handling.
