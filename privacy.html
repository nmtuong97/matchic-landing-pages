Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

Matchic is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here's what we handle:

  • Wardrobe photos: Stored only on your device. We never see them.
  • Waitlist email: Only if you choose to join. Used solely for launch updates.
  • App usage: We do not track how you use the app.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your photos to any server.
  • We do not track your behavior or location.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising cookies.

Your Choices

  • You can use Matchic without creating an account.
  • You can delete your wardrobe data at any time.
  • You can unsubscribe from waitlist emails anytime.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Matchic evolves. We will not reduce your privacy rights without notice.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at privacy@matchic.app.
