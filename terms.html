2. Description of Service

Matchic is a wardrobe management application that provides the following core features:

  • Digital wardrobe organization and management
  • AI-powered auto-tagging of clothing items (color, category, style, material)
  • AI-generated outfit recommendations based on your wardrobe
  • Lookbook creation and management
  • Backup and restore functionality
  • Batch import of clothing items

All AI processing is performed locally on your device. The App does not require an internet connection for core functionality.
