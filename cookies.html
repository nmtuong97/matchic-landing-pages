Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

In short: Matchic does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or any form of user monitoring.

1. No Cookies

The Matchic mobile application does not use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, or any similar tracking technologies. Since the app runs entirely on your device with no server-side component, there is no mechanism to set or read cookies.

2. No Tracking

Matchic does not implement any form of user tracking, including:

  • Analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising identifiers or attribution platforms
  • Session recording or heat mapping
  • Crash reporting tools that collect personal data
  • Third-party SDKs with tracking capabilities

3. Website (matchic.app)

Our landing page website is a static site hosted on a CDN. It does not set cookies, use analytics, or collect visitor data. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics services
  • Advertising cookies or retargeting pixels
  • Social media tracking buttons
  • Session cookies or persistent cookies of any kind

Our hosting provider may capture standard server logs (IP address, browser user-agent, requested page) for operational purposes such as troubleshooting and security. These logs are retained temporarily and are not used for analytics, profiling, or marketing.

4. Local Storage

The Matchic app uses the following local-only storage mechanisms on your device:

  • ObjectBox: Local NoSQL database for wardrobe data — never transmitted
  • SharedPreferences: For app preferences (language, theme) — never transmitted
  • Local file storage: For photos and backup files — never transmitted

None of these are cookies. They are purely local storage mechanisms that operate within the app's sandbox and are inaccessible to any external service.

5. Do Not Track (DNT)

Because Matchic does not track users in any way, "Do Not Track" browser signals are inherently respected. Our app and website have no tracking mechanisms to disable.

6. Third-Party Services

The only external network request the app makes is downloading the AI model (~275MB) from Google's official repository, which you explicitly opt into. This is a standard file download and does not involve cookies, tracking, or data collection.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy:

  • Email: support@matchic.app
