About Matchic

Version 1.0.0

What is Matchic?

Matchic is an AI-powered wardrobe management application that helps you digitize, organize, and get style recommendations for your clothing — all while keeping your data completely private on your device.

Built with Flutter and running Google's Gemma AI model locally via MediaPipe, Matchic brings intelligent fashion assistance to your pocket without compromising your privacy.

Why Matchic?

Most wardrobe apps require cloud accounts, upload your photos to servers, or use third-party AI services that process your data externally. Matchic is different:

  • 100% on-device: AI runs locally. No cloud, no uploads.
  • No account required: Download and start using immediately.
  • Offline-first: All features work without internet.
  • Open source: Our code is public on GitHub for full transparency.

Key Features

  • AI Auto-Tagging: Take or upload a photo; AI automatically identifies color, category, style, and material
  • Smart Outfit Matching: Scan any item for AI-generated outfit recommendations
  • Digital Closet: Browse, search, filter, and sort your wardrobe with ease
  • Lookbook: Save and organize your favorite outfit combinations
  • Batch Import: Add multiple items at once to digitize your wardrobe quickly
  • Backup & Restore: Export your entire wardrobe as a ZIP file
  • Bilingual: Full support for English and Vietnamese

Tech Stack

Flutter & Dart Clean Architecture BLoC State Management ObjectBox Database MediaPipe GenAI Gemma 3 Model AutoRoute Liquid Glass UI

Platform Support

  • iOS: iOS 16.0+ (iPhone, iPad)
  • Android: Android 8.0 (API 26)+

Open Source

Matchic is open source under the MIT License. You can view, audit, and contribute to the source code on GitHub. We believe transparency is essential for a privacy-focused application — anyone can verify that we do exactly what we promise with your data.
