Frequently Asked Questions

Is Matchic really private?

Yes. Matchic processes everything on your device. The AI runs locally via MediaPipe, and all your wardrobe data is stored in ObjectBox on your phone. We do not collect, store, or have access to your data. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

How does the AI tagging work?

When you add a clothing item by taking a photo or selecting from your gallery, Matchic uses an on-device AI model (Gemma 3 via MediaPipe) to analyze the image. It identifies colors, suggests categories (top, bottom, shoes, etc.), detects patterns and materials, and recommends style tags — all without sending your image anywhere.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, all core features work completely offline. The only time you need internet is if you choose to download the AI model for the first time (~275MB download from Google's official repository), or to perform a Backup & Restore via cloud storage.

How do I back up my wardrobe?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore. You can export your entire wardrobe (including photos and data) as a ZIP file to your device. The backup can be restored later on the same or a different device. We recommend backing up regularly.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

All locally stored data will be removed. We strongly recommend exporting a backup before uninstalling. After reinstalling, you can restore from your backup file to get everything back.

Which platforms are supported?

Matchic is built with Flutter and supports both iOS and Android. Minimum requirements:

  • iOS: iOS 16.0 or later
  • Android: Android 8.0 (API 26) or later, with a device that supports on-device AI inference (most modern smartphones)

How do I download the AI model?

When you first use AI features (auto-tagging or outfit recommendation), the app will prompt you to download the Gemma 3 model (~275MB). This one-time download happens from Google's official repository. After that, all AI features work fully offline.

Is Matchic free?

Matchic is free to download and use. There are no subscription fees or in-app purchases for core features. Future premium features may be introduced, but we will always keep the core functionality free and private.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! Matchic is open source under the MIT License. You can contribute via GitHub — report bugs, suggest features, or submit pull requests. Check out our GitHub repository to get started.

How do I request a feature?

Feature requests are welcome! Please open a discussion on our GitHub Discussions page. Include as much detail as possible about what you'd like to see and why it would be useful.
