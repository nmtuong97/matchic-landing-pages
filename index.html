Your Privacy, Our Priority

Built for Privacy from the Ground Up

Matchic processes everything on your device. No servers, no uploads, no tracking.

📱

100% On-Device

AI runs locally using Google's Gemma model via MediaPipe. No cloud dependency.

🚫

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or transmit your wardrobe data. Everything stays on your phone.

🔓

Open Source

Our code is open for audit. Transparency is built into our DNA.

📷

Camera Privacy

Photos are processed locally and never uploaded. Camera access is only used when you actively take a photo.
