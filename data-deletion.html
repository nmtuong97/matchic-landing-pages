Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Important: Matchic stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect or store any data on our servers. Therefore, data deletion is under your direct control.

Option 1: Delete All Data Within the App

  1. Open Matchic on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Clear All Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears

⚠️ This action is irreversible. We recommend exporting a backup first if you wish to keep your data for future use.

Option 2: Delete Individual Items

  1. Open Matchic and browse your closet
  2. Tap on any clothing item to open its detail view
  3. Tap the delete option
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 3: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Matchic will automatically delete all locally stored data, including:

  • All clothing photos and metadata
  • Lookbook entries and saved outfits
  • App preferences and settings
  • AI model files (if downloaded)
  • Backup files stored in the app's private directory

Note: If you have previously exported a backup ZIP file to your device's shared storage (Downloads/Documents folder), that file will remain after uninstallation. You can manually delete it using your device's file manager.

Option 4: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete your data using the options above, you can contact us to request assistance:

  • Email: support@matchic.app
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your device model and OS version (for troubleshooting)

Please note: Since we do not collect or store your data on any server, there is nothing for us to delete from our end. We will guide you through the on-device deletion process.

What Data Gets Deleted vs. Retained

Data Type Deleted? Notes
Clothing photos ✅ Yes Deleted from app storage
Item metadata ✅ Yes Deleted from ObjectBox
Lookbook/saved outfits ✅ Yes Deleted from ObjectBox
AI model ✅ Yes Deleted with app data
Backup ZIP files ⚠️ Manual Delete manually from device storage
Server-side data ✅ N/A No server-side data exists

California & GDPR Users

If you are a California resident or GDPR-protected user, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Given our on-device architecture, you can exercise this right directly through the methods above. For any questions related to your deletion rights, contact us at support@matchic.app with the subject "Data Deletion Request."

We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days as required by applicable regulations.

Confirmation

Once data deletion is completed (via in-app Clear All Data or uninstallation), no further confirmation is needed — your data is permanently removed from the device. If you contacted us via email, we will confirm that we have guided you through the deletion process within 30 days.
